# Class 6 Learning Platform - User Guide

## Welcome! 🎓

This is your complete learning companion for Class 6, covering all subjects with interactive lessons, activities, and quizzes.

---

## Quick Start

### For Students

1. **Open the App**: Visit the homepage
2. **Choose a Subject**: Click on any subject card (English, Math, Science, etc.)
3. **Select a Topic**: Browse chapters and click on a topic to learn
4. **Learn**: Read the lesson, try activities, and practice exercises
5. **Take Quiz**: Test your knowledge with 10 questions
6. **Track Progress**: See your completion percentage for each subject

---

## Features Overview

### 🎯 Three Learning Modes

#### 1. **📖 Learn Tab**

- Ask questions about the topic
- Get helpful responses

#### 3. **🧠 Quiz Tab**

- 3 types: Multiple Choice, Short Answer, Application
- Immediate feedback after each answer

## How to Use Each Feature

### Reading a Lesson

1. Click on a topic from the subject page
2. The **Learn tab** opens automatically
3. Scroll through the content:
   - Read the concept explanation
   - Look at real-life examples
   - Try the hands-on activities (if available)
   - Practice "Try It" exercises
   - Discuss "Think & Discuss" questions with friends or family

### Using Voice Features

**Listen to Lesson:**
- Click the **🔊 Read Lesson Aloud** button
- The computer will read the entire lesson to you
- Works in English, Hindi, and Kannada

**Ask Questions with Voice:**
- Click the microphone icon
- Speak your question
- The app will switch to the Chat tab with your question

### Taking a Quiz

1. Go to the **Quiz tab**
2. Click **🚀 Start Quiz**
3. Answer each question:
   - **MCQ**: Click on your answer choice
   - **Short Answer**: Type your answer in the box
   - **Application**: Explain how you would apply the concept
4. Click **Check Answer** to see if you're correct
5. Read the explanation to learn more
6. Click **Next Question** to continue
7. After 10 questions, see your final score!

**Scoring:**
- Get 60% or more (6 out of 10) to complete the topic ✅
- Less than 60%? No problem! Click **🔄 Retry** to try again

### Tracking Your Progress

**On Subject Page:**
- See a progress bar showing % completion
- Topics you've completed have a ✓ checkmark
- Topics with quiz scores show your score

**On Topic Page:**
- "Completed" badge appears when you finish
- Click **Mark as Done** to manually mark complete

## Tips for Success

### 📖 Learning Tips
1. **Read Carefully**: Don't rush through the content
2. **Do the Activities**: Hands-on learning helps you remember
3. **Try the Exercises**: Practice makes perfect
4. **Discuss**: Talk about "Think & Discuss" questions with others
5. **Use Voice**: Listen to lessons while doing other activities

### 🧠 Quiz Tips
1. **Read Questions Carefully**: Make sure you understand what's being asked
2. **Think Before Answering**: Don't rush
3. **Read Explanations**: Even if you got it right, read the explanation
4. **Retry if Needed**: It's okay to take the quiz multiple times
5. **Learn from Mistakes**: Use wrong answers as learning opportunities

### 🎯 Progress Tips
1. **Set Goals**: Try to complete 1-2 topics per day
2. **Review Regularly**: Go back to completed topics to refresh
3. **Track Progress**: Check your subject completion percentage
4. **Stay Consistent**: A little bit every day is better than cramming
5. **Celebrate Success**: Feel proud when you complete a subject!

## NEP 2020 Learning Approach

This platform follows India's National Education Policy 2020 principles:

### 🛠️ Experiential Learning
- Hands-on activities you can do at home
- Real experiments with everyday materials
- Learning by doing, not just reading

### 💭 Critical Thinking
- "Think & Discuss" questions make you analyze
- Application questions test real understanding
- No rote memorization required

### 🌍 Real-World Connection
- Every topic has real-life examples
- Scenarios show how concepts apply to daily life
- Learning is relevant and practical

### 🎯 Competency-Based
- 60% mastery threshold ensures understanding
- Can retry quizzes until you master the topic
- Progress based on learning, not just completion

### 🗣️ Multilingual
- Content in English, Hindi, and Kannada
- Voice support for all languages
- Learn in the language you're comfortable with

## Troubleshooting

### Quiz not loading?
- Refresh the page
- Make sure JavaScript is enabled
- Try a different browser

### Voice not working?
- Check your browser supports Web Speech API (Chrome, Edge work best)
- Make sure your device has speakers/headphones
- Check volume settings

### Progress not saving?
- Make sure cookies/LocalStorage is enabled
- Don't use private/incognito mode
- Use the same browser and device

### Content not displaying?
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Check internet connection (for first load)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How many questions are in each quiz?**  
A: Every topic has exactly 10 questions.

**Q: What score do I need to pass?**  
A: You need 60% (6 out of 10) to complete a topic.

**Q: Can I retake a quiz?**  
A: Yes! You can retake quizzes as many times as you want.

**Q: Does this work offline?**  
A: After the first load, most content works offline. Progress is saved locally.

**Q: Can I use this on my phone?**  
A: Yes! The platform works on phones, tablets, and computers.

**Q: Is there a time limit for quizzes?**  
A: No, take as much time as you need.

**Q: Can I skip topics?**  
A: Yes, you can learn topics in any order.

**Q: How is my progress saved?**  
A: Progress is saved automatically in your browser's local storage.

**Q: Can multiple students use the same device?**  
A: Yes, but they'll share the same progress. Use different browsers or profiles for separate progress.

**Q: Are there video lessons?**  
A: Not yet, but you can use the voice reading feature to listen to lessons.
